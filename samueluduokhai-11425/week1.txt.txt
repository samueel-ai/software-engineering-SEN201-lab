A software engineer is a professional who applies the principles of engineering to the design, development, testing, and maintenance of software systems. They use programming languages and problem-solving techniques to create computer programs that perform specific tasks or solve real-world problems.

Software engineers work on different types of software, such as mobile applications, websites, operating systems, and computer games. They analyze user needs, design solutions, write code, and ensure that the software is efficient, reliable, and easy to use.
